## ðŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

**Images not showing?**
- You're in Low-Bandwidth Mode (saves data)
- Click image label to load on-demand
- Or toggle Low-Bandwidth Mode off

**Can't load new courses?**
- You're offline
- Check your internet connection
- Previously loaded courses remain available

**Page won't update?**
- Hard refresh: `Ctrl+F5` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac)
- Clear cache if issues persist
